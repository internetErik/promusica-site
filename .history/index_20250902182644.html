We sell music

audio equipment is simply a means to that end

Promusica offers a listening environment where you can take your time and make informed decisions, based on your own observations and tastes. Our goal is to offer quality, value & commitment to our clients, through our knowledge and expertise of the best audio technologies available today.

our story

Pro Musica was co-founded by Ken Christianson & John Schwarz in 1983. Founded to share both our passion for music & fine audio equipment.

Our goal is offering quality, value & commitment to our clients through our continued research of both recording & playback with the best audio technologies available.

services

design & logical system upgrades

We can help you make informed decisions – based on your own observations. Our innovative system designs combine the best of new & old technologies, responding to the unique characteristics of individual subjective listening styles, room specifics, and budgets big & small.

Complete installation for all systems. We can suggest optimal system setup, placement, and acoustical treatments to optimize your system playback for the best sound in your rooms at home.
